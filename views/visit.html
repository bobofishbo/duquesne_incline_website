Parking Information

Parking is available at both the upper and lower stations:

Lower Station Parking

Limited metered street parking is available on W. Carson Street. Additionally, a paid parking lot is located directly across from the lower station entrance.

  • Daily Rate: $8.00
  • Evening Rate (after 5pm): $5.00

Upper Station Parking

Free parking is available in the lot adjacent to the upper station on Grandview Avenue. This lot has approximately 20 spaces and is available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Additional street parking can be found along Grandview Avenue.

Public Transportation Options

The Duquesne Incline is accessible via several Port Authority bus routes. The following bus lines stop within walking distance of the lower station:

  • Route 18 - Manchester
  • Route 20 - Kennedy
  • Route 21 - West End
  • Route 22 - McCoy

For the latest route information and schedules, please visit the Port Authority website.

Visitor Tips

Best Times to Visit

To avoid crowds, visit on weekdays or before 11am and after 7pm on weekends. Sunset offers the most spectacular views of the city.

Photography Tips

The upper observation deck provides panoramic views of Pittsburgh's skyline. Early evening offers the best lighting for photography.

Accessibility

The Duquesne Incline is wheelchair accessible at both the upper and lower stations. Please ask our staff for assistance if needed.

Museum & Exhibits

Don't miss the museum at the upper station, featuring historical photos and mechanical displays explaining how the incline operates.

Nearby Attractions

Grandview Avenue

Enjoy spectacular views of Pittsburgh's skyline from this scenic overlook.

Point State Park

Visit the historic fountain where the three rivers meet, just a short drive from the incline.

Dining Options

Enjoy a meal with a view at one of the many restaurants along Grandview Avenue.
